Important Rules

  • Your regex must contain a named group: (?P<episode>\d+)
  • If you provide a rename pattern, it must contain: {episode_pattern}
  • The placeholder {episode_pattern} will be replaced by the extracted episode number

Common Patterns

(?P<episode>\d+) Simple digit capture
\.S01E(?P<episode>\d+)\. Season 01 with episode
S\d{2}E(?P<episode>\d+) Flexible season number

Rename Pattern Examples

SeriesName S01E{episode_pattern}.mkv
ShowName - {episode_pattern} - Title.mp4
{episode_pattern}.avi
